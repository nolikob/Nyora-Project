    The keeper of your promises

    Nyora is web based web application, helps you be more organized and not lose a track with events you planned to be on or even work on. Nyora is here to do two things. To keep events you've planned to be displayed within the app and to give you an option of a to-do list. You can add multiple things in to one day and then even download it in JSON and use it elsewhere as an data source. How cool is that ?

    Once you log in, user friendly lightweight enviroment is presented to you. Everything is thought to be intuitive, but there is hint section that'll help you.

    Please bear in mind that this is only one guy project who's just started studying this branch of IT. I might've taken a big bite and I apologize if this page is not what you would've expected.
